The Rabinovich-Fabrikant attractor

(Using the Runge-Kutta 4th-order method)

The Rabinovich-Fabrikant system

$$ \begin{aligned} \frac{dx}{dt} & = y(z - 1 + x^2) + \gamma x \\ \frac{dy}{dt} & = x(3z + 1 - x^2) + \gamma y \\ \frac{dz}{dt} & = -2z(\alpha + xy) \\ \end{aligned} $$

Parameters

$$ \begin{aligned} \alpha & = 1.1 \\ \gamma & = 0.87 \\ \end{aligned} $$

Initial conditions

$$ \begin{aligned} (x_0, y_0, z_0) & = (-1.05, 0.9, 1.01) \\ \text{Step size: }h & = 0.001 \\ t & = 50000 \end{aligned} $$
