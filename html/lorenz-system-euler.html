The Lorenz attractor

(Using the Euler method)

The Lorenz system

$$ \begin{aligned} \frac{dx}{dt} & = \sigma(y - x) \\ \frac{dy}{dt} & = x(\rho - z) - y \\ \frac{dz}{dt} & = xy - \beta z \\ \end{aligned} $$

Parameters

$$ \begin{aligned} \sigma & = 10.0 \\ \rho & = 28.0 \\ \beta & = 8.0/3.0 \\ \end{aligned} $$

Initial conditions

$$ \begin{aligned} (x_0, y_0, z_0) & = (1, 0, 0) \\ \text{Step size: } h & = 0.01 \\ t & = 10000 \end{aligned} $$
