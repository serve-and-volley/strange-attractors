The Lorenz-84 attractor

(Using the Runge-Kutta 4th-order method)

The Lorenz-84 system

$$ \begin{aligned} \frac{dx}{dt} & = -ax - y^2 - z^2 + aF \\ \frac{dy}{dt} & = -y + xy -bxz + G \\ \frac{dz}{dt} & = -z + bxy + xz \\ \end{aligned} $$

Parameters

$$ \begin{aligned} a & = 1.111 \\ b & = 1.479 \\ F & = 4.494 \\ G & = 0.44 \end{aligned} $$

Initial conditions

$$ \begin{aligned} (x_0, y_0, z_0) & = (1, 1, 1) \\ \text{Step size: }h & = 0.136 \\ t & = 3500 \end{aligned} $$
